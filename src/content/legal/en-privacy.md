## What data we collect

When you use SkillTreq, we collect the following information:

- **Account information**: Email address and basic profile data from your OAuth provider (GitHub or Google)
- **Workout data**: Exercise logs, sets, reps, holds, and personal records you enter
- **Progress data**: Skill statuses, training plan progress, and achievement timestamps
- **Usage data**: Anonymous analytics about feature usage to improve the product

## How we store your data

- All data is stored in **Neon PostgreSQL** databases
- The application is hosted on **Vercel** infrastructure
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest

## Third-party services

SkillTreq uses the following third-party services:

- **GitHub OAuth** — for account authentication
- **Google OAuth** — for account authentication
- **Brevo** — for transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
- **Sentry** — for error tracking and performance monitoring

We do not sell your data to third parties. Third-party services only receive the minimum data necessary to provide their functionality.

## Cookies and local storage

- **Session cookie** — used for authentication (httpOnly, secure)
- **localStorage** — used for UI preferences (theme, language)
- **No tracking cookies** — we do not use advertising or analytics cookies

## Data retention

- Your data is retained for as long as your account is active
- If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days
- Anonymous, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely

## Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — view all your information within the app
- **Export** your data — use the JSON export feature in app settings
- **Delete** your data — delete your account in app settings, which removes all associated data
- **Correct** your data — update your profile and workout data at any time

## Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at **privacy@skilltreq.com**.
