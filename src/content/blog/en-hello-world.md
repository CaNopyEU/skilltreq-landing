## Why a blog

SkillTreq is built in the open. This blog is where we share what we're working on, why we make certain decisions, and what we learn along the way.

You'll find three types of content here:

- **Devlogs** — regular updates on what changed in the app and why
- **Guides** — practical content about calisthenics progressions and how to use SkillTreq effectively
- **Roadmap updates** — where the project is heading and what's next

## Where we are now

SkillTreq is in open Beta. The core is working — skill trees with dependency graphs, active workout sessions, training plans, and progress tracking. We're using it ourselves and refining it based on real training data.

The landing site you're reading this on was just rebuilt with a proper blog system, automated content pipeline, and multi-language support (English, Slovak, Czech). Every post goes through a validator that checks for hype language and brand voice compliance before publishing.

## What to expect

We write for athletes who care about real data, not hype. No motivational fluff, no gamification language, no fake urgency. Just honest updates about building a training tool that works.

Posts will come roughly every two weeks, timed with development sprints. Most will be devlogs — short, focused updates on what changed and why.
